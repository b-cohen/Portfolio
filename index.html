Biography

Hello, I am Brittany Cohen and the current technical director at Base Camp Coding Academy (BCCA). Base Camp Coding Academy is a non profit organization that provide students the opportunity to become full stack web developers. Base camp is located in Water Valley, MS and tuition is 100% free for all of our students.Base Camp Coding Academy is a hands-on, challenging, and fun program designed to train recent high school graduates to be software engineers. In an intimate classroom setting, students work with real world technologies to learn the fundamentals of coding, app development and the life leadership skills they will need to be successful in their career and competitive in the job market upon graduation. To learn more about Base Camp Coding Academy please refer to the link on the right in the navigation bar.

Before becoming the technical director, I was the Engineering and Exploring Computer Science instructor for the North Panola School District. I have five years of experience at North Panola Career and Technical Center. During my tenure in public education, I was able to partner with researchers from the University of California Los Angeles(UCLA). Our research was focused on equity in computer science especially in the Mississippi Delta. I had the pleasure to collaborate with Dr. Jean Ryoo UCLA researcher and Jane Margolis author of Stuck in the Shallow End: Education, Race, and Computing for several years in the classroom.

In 2018, I was able to be assist in writing the exploring computer science curriculum for the state of Mississippi. I also help write several Science Technology Engineering Mathematics (STEM) grants for North Panola. In 2020, I was named Teacher of the Year by the Panola County Partnership.
